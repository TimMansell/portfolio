A little bit about myself

I am an experienced and ambitious Front-end Developer with over 6 years commercial experience in a design agency environment. I excell in both front-end and back-end development.

I develop with best practices in mind and adhere to W3C standards. Not all browsers are created equal, so I ensure that any website I work on displays pixel-perfect in all of the major players (Chrome, Firefox, IE & Safari).

I believe that having a functional website is only part of the solution. Being found is just as important. Search Engine Optimisation is an integral part of my development process.
